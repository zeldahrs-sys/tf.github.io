Guide notes

Relics used in the original route: Endless harvest, Woodsman, Evil eye, Butler's bell, Larcenist, Culling spree, Reload: Soul harvest, Minion.

  • The route leans in favor of those relics, so some adjustments may be needed if you choose differently.
  • The amount of items to buy is intentional, especially early on.
  • Do not buy more than mentioned because carried coin amounts can matter.
